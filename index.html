About the Project

WuXuxian TTRPG is an open-source tabletop role-playing game that brings the rich traditions of Chinese martial arts fantasy to your gaming table. Inspired by classics like Romance of the Three Kingdoms, Journey to the West, and modern cultivation novels, players can create unique characters who walk the path of cultivation toward immortality.

The system features innovative mechanics for cultivation paths, soul cores, domain sources, and a unique fate card system that shapes your character's destiny. Whether you seek martial supremacy, spiritual enlightenment, or political intrigue, WuXuxian offers deep, rewarding gameplay.
