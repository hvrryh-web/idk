# Romance of the Three Kingdoms Visual Novel Style Bible

## Art Direction

- **Motifs:** Late Eastern Han / Three Kingdoms, jade, lacquer, carved wood, brass, parchment, silk, ink-wash overlays
- **Texture:** Parchment, silk, brocade, lacquered wood, cloud motifs, calligraphic flourishes
- **Lighting:** Soft gradients, painterly rim light, bloom, vignette, atmospheric haze

## Palette

- **Authority:** Imperial reds, golds
- **Strategy:** Jade, teal
- **UI Panels:** Parchment, ink tones
- **Battlefield:** Earth, steel, vivid aura highlights
- **Yuto Sano cues:** Soft gradients, luminous rim light, bloom

## Typography

- **Headers:** Brush-style, serif
- **Body:** Clean sans-serif
- **Effects:** Stroke outlines, shadows for legibility

## UI Components

- Scroll panels, jade plaques, banners, stamps, buttons, cursors
- Borders: Brushstroke, lacquered frames
- Status: Calligraphic stamps, color-coded borders

## Map & Screen Concepts

- **Map Screen:** Ink-wash world map, parchment, animated clouds, lacquered province plaques
- **War Map:** Tabletop diorama, lantern lighting, toggles for supply/morale/weather/elevation
- **City Screen:** Walled city overview, lantern pins, bamboo strip tooltips
- **Regional Map:** Ink-stroke roads, heatmap, margin stamps
- **World Map:** Atlas frame, fog-of-war, animated banners
- **Personal View:** Ground-level vignette, silk scroll dialogue, painterly busts
- **Conversation:** Parallax busts, animated cloth/flags, brushstroke FX, jade plaques for choices

## Asset Pipeline (ComfyUI)

- **Character Portraits:** Semi-realistic, ornate Han armor/robes, painterly gradients, rim light, Yuto Sano style
- **Backgrounds:** Ink-wash + semi-realistic, depth maps for parallax, bloom, vignette
- **Maps & Icons:** Parchment/ink base, vector icons, lacquered frames, jade plaques
- **FX & Overlays:** Brushstroke slashes, aura glows, embers, petals, bloom, chromatic drift
- **Consistency:** LUT/grain/vignette stack, color balance audit, prompt presets

## Accessibility

- High-contrast scrolls, scalable text, outlines, SFX toggles

## Deliverables

- Palette swatches, typography samples, border/icon set, lighting references
- Asset packs: characters, backgrounds, FX overlays
- UI components, map pack, integration guide
